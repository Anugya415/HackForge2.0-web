2. Sign up with a new user (e.g., "Workflow User", "workflow@example.com", "password123").

3. After successful signup/login, verify redirection to the Dashboard.
4. Locate the Sidebar on the left.
5. Verify that the user name "Workflow User" is visible in the sidebar.
6. Verify that the email "workflow@example.com" is **NOT** visible in the sidebar.
7. Report the findings.
